Company Information

In January 1996, Panasonic Gulf FZE (PGF) was founded in Dubai as the first sales company in the Middle East and resumed exports to the region's neighboring countries.
In line with the localization policy for transferring export sales activities near the actual market, in 2001 Panasonic Marketing Middle East FZE (PMM) was founded in Dubai as the regional marketing headquarters for the Middle East.
In 2012, with the expansion of its scope to cover the African market, the company name was changed to Panasonic Marketing Middle East & Africa FZE (PMMAF).
